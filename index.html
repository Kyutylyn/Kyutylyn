Interest

I have interest in writing ever since I was a kid, elementary to be exact. At first I have this hobbies of reading textbooks, I always find joy and peace whenever I read. I love reading so much that I could sit the whole day just to read. I only love to read but I never had imagine myself writing a story. I remembered the first ever story that I've wrote,It was on third grade. Funny how it started from jealousy and envy, and now it became my passion. The funny reason why I started writing it's because, I was jealous of my classmates, they were too good when It comes to arts. They were able to draw disney princesses while I myself can't even draw a perfect circle. And that's where I come up to an idea of writing, at first no one could appreciate it,because my penmanship sucks.

Projects
